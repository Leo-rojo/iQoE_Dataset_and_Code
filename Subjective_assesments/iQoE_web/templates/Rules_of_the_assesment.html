Training of Your Personalized QoE Model

To construct a personalized Quality of Experience (QoE) model for your individual perception of video quality, this website will present 120 video clips and ask you to score them.

  • If you use the site for the first time, press the "Start a New Session" button.
  • If you want to continue with an earlier unfinished session, press the "Resume an Earlier Session" button.

Please be aware that:

  • The long duration and repetitive pattern of watching the video clips and providing QoE scores might cause psychological stress and tiredness.
  • The video clips, extracted from the short film "Tears of Steel", might contain sensitive content.
  • In order to resume a paused session, we use cookies that contain no personal information and expire after 3 days.

By clicking "Accept and Start a New Session" or "Accept and Resume an Earlier Session", you are confirming that you have read and agree to terms and conditions of this assessment.
